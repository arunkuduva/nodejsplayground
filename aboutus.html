About Us

Millennium Software Consulting, LLC is an Information Technology Management and Consulting services company with offices in the US and India. Formed by some of the industry's most experienced and knowledgeable people, MSC is growing to be one of the best-managed consulting companies in the world. MSC has established itself as a reliable supplier of IT services. We specialize in IT staff augmentation, On-site and Off-site IT consulting, Turnkey Project Outsourcing, and eBusiness Solutions. Our software professionals have successfully worked on many on-site and off-site IT consulting engagements across the US. Our business focus includes areas of on-site, off-site and offshore information technology consulting services and software development. The software development centers in our corporate office at Charlottesville, VA and at Chennai, India are well equipped to undertake software development, maintenance and conversion activities.

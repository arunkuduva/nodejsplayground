ERP Solutions

MSC believes that basic idea behind ERP is efficiency. ERP software can streamline the inner workings of a business by letting each individual business unit share information in a common standardized way. But the time it takes the different approaches, and the sometimes-colossal cost, can leave some decision-makers befuddled when it comes to ERP.

MSC identifies the vital considerations in ERP package selection starts with a clear articulation of the business problems being addressed the reason a company is looking for an ERP solution is that one of its three core corporate functions need to be fixed. In other words, its financial data is not integrated, or it needs to standardize its manufacturing processes across several business units, or its human resources area needs some heavy-duty reengineering. Depending upon which of the three is the critical function, certain enterprise software offerings may be better suited to meet those needs.

MSC brings a powerful combination of experience, strong partnerships, industry expertise, technology leadership and global delivery to each of enterprise software projects.

Our PeopleSoft, Oracle, Lawson, SAP, Supply Chain and ERP Integration practices place strong emphasis on partnerships to deliver excellent value and bottom-line results for our global customers. We focus on providing unique solutions that help reduce the time and cost of enterprise software projects to clients in an wide range of business sectors, including Public Sector, A&D, Healthcare, Services and Industrial.

We also continue to provide the functional application expertise, technical knowledge, and project management know-how our customers have come to expect, with skills, templates, methodologies, and ingenuity that enable us to deliver an on-time, production-ready system on time and within your budget.
